- Assumption Math Opp. for many Numbers 
For Example: the sample is *4 | 8 | 3 | 6 | 2 | 60*  --> you should find *987* with math opperations. 
the solution: 
<l>2*8 =16 </l>
<l>16*60 = 960  </l>
<l>6*4 = 24 </l>
<l>24+3 = 27  </l>
<l>960+27 = 987 </l>
