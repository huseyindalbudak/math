2. Assumption math opperations :1234: islemGames.py for many Numbers 
For Example: the sample is *4 | 8 | 3 | 6 | 2 | 60*  --> you should find *987* with math opperations. 
the solution: 
<li>2*8 =16 </li>
<li>16*60 = 960  </li>
<li>6*4 = 24 </li>
<li>24+3 = 27  </li>
<li>960+27 = 987 </li>
